My Focus

I like to keep my design sweet, simple, and intuitive. To me, a good website is one that effortlessly delivers on its function, and then looks good - not the other way around.

Projects With an Elegant Simplcity in mind.

Project Portfolio This page!

The intention here was to deliver the core content and functionality in a minimalistic and pleasing way.

The core content is comprised of a hearty greeting, basic information about me as a web-dev, and a showcase of my projects. Desiring an ability to talk a bit about these projects while keeping clutter to a minimum, I worked out the current thumbnail system.

The core functionality is comprised of ability to contact me. Because I'll likely want to send replies, I've only provided contact methods that allow me this capability. There's also the navigation bar, which I feel is un-needed due to the small page size, but I've kept for convention's sake.

The visual style expresses as much as possible in as little as possible. To do thi,s I've kept text to the meaningful minimum and focused heavily on visibility of what is present.
